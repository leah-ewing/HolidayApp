Meet the Developer
Hi there! I'm Leah Ewing, a junior full-stack engineer and the sole developer behind HolidayApp. Fueled by a passion for coding and celebrations, I ventured into creating HolidayApp, a platform born out of my love for crafting delightful experiences.
What It Is
HolidayApp is your ultimate calendar app, revealing the unique holiday being celebrated on any given day. Utilizing Flask for the backend and HTML/CSS for the frontend, I've meticulously crafted this platform. The robustness of our database, powered by PostgreSQL and SQLAlchemy, ensures seamless management of celebrations and user experiences. If you sign up, HolidayApp will send you daily emails, keeping you updated on the day's special holiday. Leveraging AI technology, for holiday blurbs and daily emails (even this "About Me" page!), allows me to ensure that every aspect of your experience is professionally written and engaging; because, speaking for writing, well, let's just say "thank goodness" for AI, swooping in to save the day when my writing skills could use a debugging session!
My Commitment
As a one-person team, I'm dedicated to crafting a technically proficient and seamless experience for every user. Your joy, celebration, and user experience matter most to me, and I'm continuously refining HolidayApp to ensure it brings a smile to your face every day, both technically and experientially.
